WHO AM I

People around me usually called me Phet ( Means diamond in Thai ). I was born as raised in Bangkok, Thailand. I completed my primary education in Kerala, India. And now I am currently pursuing my final year in Bachelor of Science degree in Software Engieering at Mae Fah Luang University, Chiang Rai, Thailand. I came to Chiang Rai because I hate to live in such a big and crowded cities like Bangkok and I love trees

I'm not exactly sure how I was introduced into the world of programming, but I know that I have completely fell in love with it. If I wasn't to be a programmer, I guess I would selling noodles or a farmer ( Seriously ). I always love to keep updating on new technologies. I'm now into the world of web development, but who knows where I might be in the future.

Aside from programming - running, football, reading, photography and playing games ( Mostly strategy games ) are what I usually do to relax myself during my free hours. I started running when I decide quit almost 6 years of smoking and I realised that I wasn't that good at football, lol. I can fluently read, write and speak both English and Thai ( Although, I'm not the best at writing ). I wish that, one day I would try hard enough to learn Chinese ( Although, I hate linguistics ).
